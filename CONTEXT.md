# Company Website

This context defines the language and intent for a company website that serves potential customers, AI/search crawlers, and job candidates.

## Language

**Yingsheng**:
A digital marketing and digital channel construction service company serving medium and large enterprise brands.
_Avoid_: Generic agency, software vendor

**Core Positioning**:
Yingsheng is a digital marketing and user operations service provider for medium and large brands, helping brands achieve long-term growth from public-domain acquisition to private-domain conversion through user growth, content communication, reputation building, interactive public relations, and digital channel construction.
_Avoid_: Full-service marketing agency, technology development company

**Homepage Hero Message**:
The homepage hero uses "让品牌增长，回到真实用户关系" as the memorable headline and uses the supporting copy to explain Yingsheng's six service lines and long-term growth positioning.
_Avoid_: Listing all service modules in the H1, generic agency slogan

**Company Website**:
The official digital entry point for the company, designed to generate customer inquiries, act as a GEO authority source, and support employer branding.
_Avoid_: Brochure site, landing page

**Chinese First Website**:
A first-phase website whose public content is written in Chinese while leaving room for future multilingual expansion.
_Avoid_: Bilingual launch, English microsite

**Visual Tone**:
A professional, modern, restrained visual style with enough motion and content energy to reflect digital marketing expertise.
_Avoid_: Pure technology style, flashy gradient style, traditional corporate template

**Potential Customer**:
A visitor evaluating whether to contact the company about its services or capabilities.
_Avoid_: User, traffic

**Consultation CTA**:
The primary conversion action that invites potential customers to contact Yingsheng about cooperation.
_Avoid_: Buy now, submit demand

**Consultation Form**:
A lightweight lead form that collects name, company, contact method, service interest, and demand description for cooperation inquiries.
_Avoid_: CRM system, long qualification form

**Lead Notification**:
The first-phase process for sending consultation form submissions to a designated business email, with room for future WeCom notification.
_Avoid_: CRM integration, silent storage

**Privacy Policy Page**:
A concise page explaining what personal information the consultation form collects, why it is collected, and how visitors can request correction or deletion.
_Avoid_: Hidden legal page, complex legal boilerplate

**Advantage Industry**:
An industry where Yingsheng has especially strong experience and repeated representative clients.
_Avoid_: Only industry, vertical limitation

**Target Industry**:
An industry Yingsheng serves through its core service lines, including automotive, consumer goods, finance, and tourism.
_Avoid_: Market, track

**GEO Authority Source**:
Website content structured so AI search and answer engines can crawl, understand, and cite accurate company information.
_Avoid_: AI-friendly copy, SEO article

**GEO Content Layer**:
Visible website content such as company facts, service FAQs, and scenario-based Q&A that helps both visitors and AI answer engines understand Yingsheng accurately.
_Avoid_: Hidden crawler text, keyword stuffing

**GEO FAQ Page**:
A low-prominence but publicly accessible FAQ page with readable answers and structured data that supports AI/search crawler understanding.
_Avoid_: Hidden crawler-only page, cloaking page

**Job Candidate**:
A visitor evaluating whether to apply for a role or learn about working at the company.
_Avoid_: Applicant, job seeker

**Careers Page**:
A candidate-facing page covering company introduction, business direction, team culture, growth opportunities, open-role entry points, and application channels.
_Avoid_: Recruiting system, job board

**Core Service Line**:
One of the six public business categories shown on the company website: user operations, word-of-mouth marketing, social media marketing, live streaming and short-video marketing, interactive public relations, and digital channel construction.
_Avoid_: Product, solution package

**Homepage Service Summary**:
A compact homepage presentation of each core service line using a title, one value sentence, and inline service keywords.
_Avoid_: Case list, full service detail

**Service Detail Page**:
A page for one core service line that expands the homepage summary with service content, platforms, target customers, business modules, and representative clients.
_Avoid_: Case page, product page

**Business Case**:
A structured story showing a client background, service line, challenge, solution, execution, and result or impact.
_Avoid_: Representative client, logo wall

**Case Result**:
The outcome section of a business case, expressed with public metrics when available or with qualitative impact when metrics are confidential.
_Avoid_: Empty conclusion, unverifiable claim

**Homepage Case Entry**:
A homepage section that points visitors to business cases without replacing the service-line summaries.
_Avoid_: Full case detail, client matrix

**Primary Navigation**:
The top-level website navigation: homepage, core services, service cases, Yingsheng Research Institute, Yingsheng Culture, about Yingsheng, careers, and FAQ.
_Avoid_: Product navigation, blog navigation

**Yingsheng Research Institute**:
A professional content section for industry insights, marketing methodology, platform trends, and brand growth research.
_Avoid_: Company news, generic blog

**Static Content Source**:
Markdown, JSON, or equivalent repository-managed content used for research articles, business cases, and FAQ in the first phase.
_Avoid_: CMS backend, manual database editing

**Multi-Page Website Skeleton**:
A first-phase website structure with full homepage, six service detail pages, case list and detail templates, research list and article templates, culture, about, careers, FAQ, consultation, and privacy pages.
_Avoid_: Single-page website, placeholder-only site

**Static-First Next.js Architecture**:
A Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS architecture that generates crawlable multi-page content from static content files and uses lightweight API routes for consultation form submission.
_Avoid_: Client-only SPA, CMS-first build

**About Yingsheng**:
A trust-building company facts section for customers and AI/search crawlers, covering positioning, capabilities, development history, advantage industries, customer types, contact paths, and credentials or honors when available.
_Avoid_: Culture page, company blog

**Yingsheng Culture**:
An employer-branding section covering values, team atmosphere, working style, employee activities, growth mechanisms, and office environment.
_Avoid_: Company profile, service capability

**User Operations**:
A service line that builds the full user-operation chain from public-domain acquisition to private-domain conversion to maximize user lifetime value.
_Avoid_: Private domain operations, community operations

**Word-of-Mouth Marketing**:
A service line that uses authentic interaction and opinion leader experience sharing to spread positive brand reputation among target audiences.
_Avoid_: KOL placement, PR seeding

**Social Media Marketing**:
A service line that builds brand social assets through customized communication strategies across mainstream social media platforms.
_Avoid_: Account posting, SNS operations

**Live Streaming and Short-Video Marketing**:
A service line that uses creative content, production, account operations, and live streaming execution to drive marketing conversion.
_Avoid_: Video production, livestream execution

**Interactive Public Relations**:
A service line that uses user-centered online and offline interaction to improve brand image and reputation.
_Avoid_: Event planning, PR activities

**Digital Channel Construction**:
A service line that plans, builds, and operates digital touchpoints such as websites, apps, mini programs, H5 campaigns, and business systems.
_Avoid_: Website development, software outsourcing

**GEO Optimization Service**:
A potential future service line for improving brand visibility and recommendation likelihood in AI answer engines.
_Avoid_: SEO, AI search copywriting

## Relationships

- The **Company Website** primarily serves **Potential Customers**
- **Potential Customers** should be guided toward the **Consultation CTA**
- The first phase should include a **Consultation Form** alongside direct contact methods
- **Consultation Form** submissions should use **Lead Notification** rather than first-phase CRM integration
- The **Consultation Form** should link to the **Privacy Policy Page** near the submit action
- The **Company Website** also serves as a **GEO Authority Source**
- The **GEO Authority Source** should be supported by a visible **GEO Content Layer** and technical structured data
- **GEO FAQ Page** must be accessible to human visitors and crawlers; it should not hide crawler-only content
- The **Company Website** supports **Job Candidates** without making recruiting the primary homepage goal
- The first phase is a **Chinese First Website**
- The **Company Website** should use the **Visual Tone** to balance enterprise trust with marketing-industry energy
- **Job Candidates** are served through a dedicated **Careers Page** and lightweight homepage/navigation entry points
- **Yingsheng** publicly presents six **Core Service Lines**
- **Core Positioning** unifies the six **Core Service Lines** and should guide homepage messaging
- **Homepage Hero Message** should lead with a memorable brand-growth claim and support it with the full **Core Positioning**
- The homepage presents each **Core Service Line** as a **Homepage Service Summary** without representative client names
- Representative clients belong at the end of each **Service Detail Page**, not in the homepage service summaries
- The homepage includes a **Homepage Case Entry** for selected **Business Cases**
- Every **Business Case** should include a **Case Result**, but quantified metrics are not mandatory when confidential
- The **Company Website** uses **Primary Navigation** to expose customer, GEO, employer-branding, and trust-building content
- **Yingsheng Research Institute** supports the **GEO Content Layer** with expert insight content rather than company news
- First-phase research articles, service cases, and FAQ should use a **Static Content Source** instead of a CMS backend
- The first phase should be a **Multi-Page Website Skeleton** with complete homepage and foundational content pages
- The **Multi-Page Website Skeleton** should use a **Static-First Next.js Architecture**
- **About Yingsheng** serves company trust and entity clarity, while **Yingsheng Culture** serves employer branding
- Automotive is the primary **Advantage Industry**, while consumer goods, finance, and tourism remain **Target Industries**
- **GEO Optimization Service** should influence website information architecture but should not appear as a public service line for now

## Example dialogue

> **Dev:** "Should the homepage hero lead with open roles?"
> **Domain expert:** "No. The homepage should lead with customer value, while still giving **Job Candidates** a clear path to careers content."

## Flagged ambiguities

- "GEO" is resolved as **GEO Authority Source**, meaning Generative Engine Optimization for AI/search crawler understanding and citation.
- "GEO optimization" is distinct from **GEO Authority Source**: the website should be crawlable for GEO now, while **GEO Optimization Service** is not currently shown as a seventh public business.
