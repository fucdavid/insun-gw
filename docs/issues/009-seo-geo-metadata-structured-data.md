## What to build

- [ ] Organization structured data is present where appropriate.
- [ ] FAQ structured data is present for FAQ content.
- [ ] Article structured data is present for Research Institute articles.
- [ ] Breadcrumb structured data is present for nested content where appropriate.
- [ ] No SEO/GEO behavior relies on hidden crawler-only content.
