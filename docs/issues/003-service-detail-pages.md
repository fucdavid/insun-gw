# 实现六个 Service Detail Pages

Label: `ready-for-agent`

## What to build

Build complete Service Detail Pages for the six public Core Service Lines: User Operations, Word-of-Mouth Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Live Streaming and Short-Video Marketing, Interactive Public Relations, and Digital Channel Construction. Each page should expand the homepage summary into a complete service narrative and end with representative clients and Consultation CTA.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Six Service Detail Pages are reachable from the core services area and homepage service summaries.
- [ ] Each Service Detail Page includes service overview, service content, service platforms, target customers, business modules, representative clients, and Consultation CTA.
- [ ] Representative clients appear at the end of each Service Detail Page.
- [ ] Representative clients do not appear inside Homepage Service Summaries.
- [ ] GEO Optimization Service does not appear as a seventh public service line.
- [ ] Service content is maintained through Static Content Source data.
- [ ] Pages render crawlable text, not image-only service descriptions.
