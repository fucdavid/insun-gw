# 实现 GEO FAQ Page 和结构化问答内容

Label: `ready-for-agent`

## What to build

Build a low-prominence but publicly accessible GEO FAQ Page. The FAQ should answer company, service scope, cooperation process, target customer, and GEO-relevant questions in readable language while supporting AI/search crawler understanding. It must not use hidden crawler-only content or cloaking.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] FAQ page is reachable from Primary Navigation or another public site entry.
- [ ] FAQ content is visible to human visitors and rendered as crawlable HTML.
- [ ] FAQ answers cover company facts, service scope, target customers, cooperation process, and common service questions.
- [ ] FAQ page does not hide crawler-only content.
- [ ] FAQ content is structured with clear headings and question-answer sections.
- [ ] FAQ content is maintained through Static Content Source data.
- [ ] The page design is low-prominence but still readable and accessible.
