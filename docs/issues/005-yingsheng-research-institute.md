# 实现映盛研究院内容区

Label: `ready-for-agent`

## What to build

Build Yingsheng Research Institute as a professional content section for industry insights, marketing methodology, platform trends, and brand growth research. It should support GEO Content Layer by publishing crawlable expert content and must not become a company news section.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Yingsheng Research Institute list page is reachable from Primary Navigation.
- [ ] Research article detail template is implemented.
- [ ] Article content is crawlable HTML text.
- [ ] Content model supports category or topic classification for industry insights, marketing methodology, platform trends, and brand growth research.
- [ ] The section does not present itself as company news.
- [ ] Research content is maintained through Static Content Source data.
