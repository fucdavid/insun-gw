# 实现 About Yingsheng、Yingsheng Culture 和 Careers Page

Label: `ready-for-agent`

## What to build

Build the company trust and employer-branding pages. About Yingsheng should provide company facts and entity clarity for customers and AI/search crawlers. Yingsheng Culture should communicate values, team atmosphere, working style, employee activities, growth mechanisms, and office environment. Careers Page should serve Job Candidates with company introduction, business direction, culture, growth opportunities, open-role entry points, and application channels.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] About Yingsheng page focuses on positioning, capabilities, development history, advantage industries, customer types, contact paths, and credentials or honors when available.
- [ ] Yingsheng Culture page focuses on values, team atmosphere, working style, employee activities, growth mechanisms, and office environment.
- [ ] Careers Page includes company introduction, business direction, team culture, growth opportunities, open-role entry points, and application channels.
- [ ] Homepage and navigation provide lightweight entry points to Careers without making recruiting the homepage's primary goal.
- [ ] About Yingsheng and Yingsheng Culture do not duplicate each other's purpose.
- [ ] Pages are readable and usable on desktop and mobile.
