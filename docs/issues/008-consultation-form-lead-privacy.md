# 实现 Consultation Form、Lead Notification 和 Privacy Policy Page

Label: `ready-for-agent`

## What to build

Build the Consultation Form and first-phase lead handling path. Potential Customers should be able to submit a lightweight inquiry with name, company, contact method, service interest, and demand description. The submission should trigger Lead Notification to a designated business email. The form must link to a concise Privacy Policy Page near the submit action.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Consultation/contact page includes direct contact methods and Consultation Form.
- [ ] Form fields include name, company, contact method, service interest, and demand description.
- [ ] Required field validation is clear and usable.
- [ ] Successful submission shows confirmation to the visitor.
- [ ] Failed submission shows an actionable error state.
- [ ] Submissions are sent to a designated business email through Lead Notification.
- [ ] CRM integration is not required or implemented in the first phase.
- [ ] Privacy Policy Page exists and is linked near the form submit action and in the footer.
